Eligibility

Who may qualify

Free Food Stockpile supports approved individuals and families in Hudson Valley who could use a hand putting healthy food on the table. You may qualify if you have Medicaid and:

  • Someone in your household has a serious or chronic health condition
  • You've worried about running out of food before you had money for more
  • You could benefit from home-delivered meals or grocery support
What you'll need to apply
First & last name Phone number Medicaid card Household details Consent
